Leek and Ham stew

Description:

A warm stew that will boost your energy through the roof!

Can be done with different additional veggies and/or smoked meat, such as sausages, ribs, bacon, etc.

Ingredients:

  • leek, 3 or 4 for a large bowl
  • 1 or 2 onions
  • 1 or 2 carrots
  • 5 potatoes
  • different smoked meat chunks
  • garlic to taste
  • different spices to taste (sweet paprika, black pepper, salt)

Steps:

  1. Finely clean and chop the onions and garlic if you will add some.
  2. Clean and chop the carrots and 'tatoes on same-size cubes.
  3. Clean and chop the leek aswell.
  4. In the meantime, put the smoked meat to cook in water. Once the water boils, reduce the heat and after 5-10 minutes change the water and repeat.
  5. Start frying the onion, after a few minutes add garlic.
  6. Stir and add carrots and put half a cup of warm water over it.
  7. Keep on reducing it, and after 5 minutes add potatoes and keep on stirring.
  8. Finally, add the leek. In the meantime, drain the cooked meat and also add it in.
  9. Spice it up according to your taste.
  10. Enjoy!
