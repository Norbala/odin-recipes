Spaghetti Bolognese

Description:

My childhood favourite! Heck, I am still lovin' it!

Can be done with different additional veggies, such as carrots, bell peppers, etc. Personally, I prefer pure ground beef for this one.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kilo ground beef
  • 5 to 6 big onions
  • 1 or 2 carrots
  • 2 cans of sliced tomatoes or tomato sauce
  • different veggies to taste (bell peppers, parsley,...)
  • one 500 gr pack of pasta (whatever pasta type you have/like)
  • different spices to taste (sweet paprika, black pepper, salt)

Steps:

  1. Finely clean and chop the onions and garlic if you will add some.
  2. Clean and finely chop the carrots and other veggies.
  3. You can also finely chop few slices of smoked bacon for extra taste.
  4. Start frying the onion, after a few minutes add garlic.
  5. Stir and add carrots and put half a cup of warm water over it.
  6. Keep on reducing it, and after 10 minutes add ground meat and keep on stirring.
  7. Once the meat is almost ready, add the tomato sauce. In the meantime, Put the salted water to boil for pasta.
  8. Spice up the Bolognese sauce according to your taste.
  9. Reduce the sauce for 20ish minutes and add sugar to reduce tomatoes' acidity.
  10. Cook the pasta al dente and it is ready.
  11. Enjoy!
